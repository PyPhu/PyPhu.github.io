Project : Automatic Tree Watering

Introduction

For this project, I got an idea from IOT (Internet Of Things) with the idea of ​​creating an automatic plant watering system. It notifies you on LINE when the plants need water, and you can check the status of the plants in the Blynk application. This project utilizes two sensors to detect soil moisture and weather conditions. The values are then compared to determine whether to use the relay module to water the plants.
